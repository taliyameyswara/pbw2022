Code Editor

I'm using Visual Code Studio, It's great for general text editing, nice and fast, for markup files, HTML/CSS, all sorts of things where Visual Studio is overkill. And has an extensive selection of community developed plugins, quick to find, install, test, and remove if needed. Very handy git support built-in, especially useful with working on multiple git repositories within a workspace. It's an essential tool, it's replaced sublime text, notepad++, etc. all of which were good general purpose editors I used to use.

Colors

I've always had this attraction to the color yellow and how bright, rememberable, and eye catching it was. The color of happiness, wisdom and imagination, Yellow is chosen by the mentally adventurous, searching for novelty and self-fulfillment. Yellow usually goes with a sunny and shrewd personality, with a good business head and a strong sense of humor. It is the color of intellectualism and all things to do with the mind. Yellow folks are usually clear and precise thinkers who have a good opinion of their own mental capacities and who have lofty ideals. They may at times tend to shun responsibility, preferring freedom of thought and action.

View: Stoicism

Stoicism means not being too excited in pleasure and too depressed in pain. How to navigate in every situation. Control things that are under my control rather than wasting energy for those uncontrollable misfortune I might face. And accepting what I can't change and changing what I can change.
